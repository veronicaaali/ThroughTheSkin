THROUGH THE SKIN
疼痛过后，墨水被植入皮肤，留下了无法擦除的图案，一个文身就这样成型。这份伴随着疼痛的艺术在很多人眼中是“离经叛道”的象征。但在这个时代，纹身不再是没有意义的跟风装酷，对很多纹身者来说，每个图案都独一无二，记录着属于自己的重要时刻，也将一直陪伴自己走下去。在父母那一辈看来，纹身是“小混混”的标配，在青年那一辈看来，纹身是“乖张暴戾”的性格，在那些有纹身的人看来，那只是一段记忆，一段故事而已。

After the pain subsides, ink is embedded into the skin, leaving behind an indelible pattern—a tattoo takes shape. This art, accompanied by pain, has long been seen by many as a symbol of defiance. However, in this era, tattoos are no longer just a meaningless trend or an attempt to look cool. For many tattooed individuals, each design is unique, marking an important moment in their lives and becoming a permanent companion on their journey. To the older generation, tattoos are a hallmark of troublemakers; to the younger generation, they signify a rebellious and unruly nature. But to those who bear them, tattoos are simply memories—stories etched into the skin.
